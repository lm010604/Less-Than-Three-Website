THE PROCESS

FIRST ROUND

You’ll get introduced to the group before singing some range-testing scales, going through some pitch-matching, and showing off a prepared solo for us.

SECOND ROUND (CALLBACKS)

If your audition leaves us wanting more, you’ll be invited to a callback, where you will learn one of our songs and run through it first with the group, and then in a quartet.

Hope to see you there!
