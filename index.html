About Us

<3 A Cappella, pronounced “Less Than Three”, is an a cappella group from Cornell University in Ithaca, NY open to all treble voices, regardless of gender identity. As a group founded on diversity, inclusivity, and professionalism, our mission is to make music accessible for everyone.

Less Than Three is committed to producing electrifying harmonies, engaging concerts, complex arrangements, and diverse set lists of everything from Disney to Broadway to Panic! at the Disco. Our dedication to executing technically sound, not to mention extremely entertaining, performances allow us to continually deliver fan favorites from TV shows, movies, video games, and various other pop culture instances as we continue to grow.
